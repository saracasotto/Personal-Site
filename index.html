About me

Hi, I’m Sara Casotto, a passionate web developer with a full stack background and a love for frontend design and aesthetics. I specialize in creating clean, functional, and visually appealing web solutions that deliver strategic results.

My passion for technology led me to start my journey with web development. Coding is where I truly feel at home— it’s the perfect blend of creativity and logic for me. While I enjoy working across the full stack, I have a particular fondness for the frontend, where I can combine my technical skills with my love for design. I aim to create websites that not only look beautiful but also fully express the personality of the client or brand behind them.

I’m open to collaborating with individuals and small businesses from any sector. My goal is to help bring their vision to life through simple yet powerful web solutions. When I’m not coding, you can find me studying languages—I’m particularly passionate about Japanese. As a self-professed nerd, I love diving into anything that keeps me learning and exploring.
